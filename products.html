Products

Visual Barriers is an Australian owned and operated company specialising in technology solutions used to identify elevated temperature assisting in the detection of COVID-19 and any other Virus.

Our camera technology comes in stand-alone or as a multi-unit or multi-premise solution allowing for stand-alone our centralised monitoring.

It can screen a group of people from 1 to 5 meters away, by 7 metres wide, in 0 to 50 degrees Celsius temperatures and has a false positive rate of = or - 0.3 degrees Celsius.

This reduces the need for long queues of people entering a premise and can integrate into identity management systems to disable card entry and provides local or mobile device alerting.

Our technology is the only military grade encrypted solution on the market, has ISO certifications and does only stores data locally so complies with all privacy legislation. All hardware and software products are designed and built in the United Kingdom.

EdgeVis Live is our patented video codec technology, originating from neural network research at the University of Strathclyde in Glasgow, the technology provides real-time video streaming over bandwidth-constrained or congested networks.
